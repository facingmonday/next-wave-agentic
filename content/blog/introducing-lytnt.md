## The Content Problem Nobody Talks About

Every restaurant, bar, and brand faces the same invisible bottleneck: **visual content**.

New menu item? You need a photo. Weekly special? You need a social post. Seasonal campaign? You need a dozen ad creatives. And you needed all of them yesterday.

The traditional options are slow and expensive. Hire a photographer for every product shot. Commission a designer for every social graphic. Brief a creative agency for every campaign. By the time the assets arrive, the special has ended and the moment has passed.

We built **lytnt** to solve this.

---

## What lytnt Does

lytnt is an AI image generation platform that creates visuals at scale from **ingredients and references**.

You provide what you're working with — a list of ingredients, product details, brand guidelines, reference images — and lytnt generates finished visuals in the style you need:

- **Cartoon and illustrated** — playful, eye-catching graphics for social media and signage
- **Lifelike and photographic** — product shots and food photography that look like they came from a studio
- **Ad-ready creatives** — complete with callouts, pricing, and brand elements
- **Social media posts** — sized, styled, and ready to publish

The key difference: lytnt doesn't generate one image at a time. It generates them **in bulk**. One set of inputs can produce dozens of variations — different styles, different angles, different compositions — so you always have options and never run dry.

## Why Bulk Generation Changes Everything

Most AI image tools are built for one-off generation. You write a prompt, you get an image, you tweak, you retry. It works for personal use. It doesn't work when you need 30 images by Friday.

lytnt is designed around **batch workflows**:

- **One input, many outputs** — Provide your ingredients and references once. Generate as many variations as you need.
- **Style consistency** — Every image in a batch shares the same brand voice, color palette, and visual language.
- **Variation without repetition** — Each image is distinct, but they all feel like they belong to the same campaign.
- **Instant iteration** — Don't like the direction? Adjust the reference and regenerate the entire batch.

This changes the economics of content creation entirely. Instead of paying per-asset, you invest in a workflow that produces assets on demand.

## How It Works

The workflow is intentionally simple:

### 1. Provide ingredients and references

Start with what you have. Menu items, product specs, brand colors, a logo, a reference image that captures the vibe. lytnt uses these as the foundation for generation.

### 2. Choose your style

Cartoon for playful social posts. Lifelike for product pages. Ad-ready with callouts and pricing for promotions. Mix and match across a single batch.

### 3. Generate in bulk

Hit generate and get back a batch of images — not one, but many. Different compositions, different emphasis, all on-brand and ready to use.

### 4. Publish and repeat

Pick your favorites, publish them, and come back next time you need fresh content. The workflow remembers your brand setup so repeat runs are even faster.
